## Non-Negotiable Rules

- This is a knowledge base for the China trip.
- Do not delete details merely to make the repository cleaner.
- Do not replace concrete information with vague wording such as "прочее", "и так далее", or "обсуждалось ранее".
- Preserve traceability: if a route, booking, risk, or decision changes, record the previous version, the new version, the reason, and the status.
- If data conflicts, keep both versions and record the conflict in [RISKS_AND_CHECKS.md](./RISKS_AND_CHECKS.md) or [OPEN_QUESTIONS.md](./OPEN_QUESTIONS.md).
- If data is uncertain, mark it as "требует проверки" instead of presenting it as fact.
- Do not expose personal data, exact booking/order numbers, passport data, QR codes, emails, phone numbers, or private service links.
- Place names should be written as English transliteration / English name. Chinese characters may be added in parentheses only as supporting information for taxis, maps, and hotels.
- Dates must be explicit, preferably `YYYY-MM-DD` or `29 June 2026`. Avoid relative dates like "tomorrow".
- Style must be precise, practical, and engineering-like: timings, logistics, risks, checks, and concrete actions.

## Working Style

The user wants a working route, not generic travel ideas. Do not reintroduce archived or rejected ideas such as Beijing, Longji Rice Terraces, or Zhujiajiao unless explicitly asked, and if mentioned, label them as archive/rejected.

The highest-risk route segment is Furong -> Wugongshan -> Wangxian on 2026-06-25 and 2026-06-26. Treat this as a critical logistics area.
